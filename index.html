Daniel O'Connell



About Me:
“Daniel O’Connell holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Graphic Design from Columbia College Chicago. Daniel spent years doing freelance work in the field of packaging design. He created memorable packaging for national recording artists and an international educational movie series. When not freelancing, Daniel maintained an award winning career in beverage hospitality, where he used his attention to detail and creativity to create featured cocktails in publications such as Chicago Tribune and Mandarin Quarterly. Daniel is also an invested community member who donates time and design work to many local non for profits like The Red Cross, Big Brothers Big Sisters of America, Greater Chicago Food Depository, and The Idaho Foodbank. In his free time he enjoys exploring the outdoors with his fiance and dog in Boise, Idaho.
